## Example use cases

Here are a few examples of the type of thing that Patchwork Protocol enables:

- Accrue experience and providence to NFTs you already own
  - If your NFT has achieved level 50 in a game you could sell your level fragment on a marketplace, attaching it to someone else's Patch
  - Alternatively if you sell your NFT, it also transfers Patch and level fragment ownership to the new NFT owner
- Run a target advertising campaign:
  - Drop 30% off Hot Topic coupons to all apes with a blue hoodie fragment
  - Set a rule that his coupon can only be transferred or sold to Apes with hoodies
- Make PFPs more dynamic
  - Earn fragments that can be sold or equipped, changing the appearnce of the NFT
  - Whitelist other 3rd party publishers of fragments to be equipped on your project's NFT
  - Create dynamic composition rules (e.g., only NFTs who have achieved level 30 in a battle app are allowed to equip the golden helmet)
  - Establish a marketplace for unused/duplicate/unwanted attribute fragments
